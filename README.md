# GILDED CRYSTAL
### Download [here](https://modrinth.com/mod/gilded-crystal) on modrinth!

Now some words about the mod,
Gilded Crystal adds a single powerful item to Minecraft, the Golden Amethyst, crafted from an amethyst shard surrounded by 8 gold nuggets.
<img width="626" height="297" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8e48d916-aee2-4f6b-b44b-29bda54ee731" />
Stacks up to 16!

1) **Kinetic Blink**: While in air, sneak and right-click to launch yourself in the direction you're looking. Consumes 1 shard.

2) **Treasure Sense**: Right-click any block to scan a 10 block radius for nearby Chests and Barrels. Particles will trail toward any containers found. Has a 10 second cooldown.
<img width="809" height="388" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/721c67cc-e7d2-4666-880d-6d4f6e297537" />

3) **Stasis Strike**: Left-click any mob while holding the Golden Amethyst to apply Slowness X and Weakness X for 4 seconds, freezing them in place. Consumes 1 item.

**Void Crystal**

Right-click a dragon egg with golden amethyst to turn the crystals into void crystal.
1) **Time Manipulation**: Sneak and throw a void crystal to go back in time 3 seconds, consumes 1 crystal and can be used atmax 5 times in a minute.

2) **Void Ability**: Player can walk on void if atleast 1 void crystal is in the inventory and right click the void to go back to the surface

3) **Void Realm**: Hold your right-click until the bar is finished and a dome filled with crying obsidian forms near the player with a 15-block radius, giving enemy negative effects like: Slowness, Weakness and Mining Fatigue during the duration of the realm and wither effect for first few seconds.
Lasts till 1 minute 30 seconds or when the enemy dies(whichever is sooner).
